1. Overview

Each game you are assigned several tasks you must accomplish to bring the city back from disrepair. If you accomplish all of your tasks, you gain access to the all-powerful switchboard! During your trials you will wield strange and powerful artifacts, outwit entire armies, and solve challenging puzzles. If at first your don't succeed, you may try as many times as you'd like!
